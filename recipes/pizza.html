Pizza

Description

Pizza is a delicious dish that is loved by many people. It is a flatbread that is topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings such as pepperoni, mushrooms, and olives. Pizza is typically baked in an oven and is a popular dish in many countries around the world. It is often served as a quick and easy meal and is perfect for sharing with friends and family.

I discovered that it takes not very much effort to make a very good pizza at home. The dough is easy to make and can be customized with your favorite toppings. You can also experiment with different types of cheese and sauces to create your own unique pizza recipe. I hope you enjoy making and eating this delicious dish as much as I do!

Ingredients

Pre-Dough

  • 1kg 00 flour
  • 500ml water
  • 5g dry yeast

Final Dough

  • Pre-Dough
  • 200ml water
  • 20g salt
  • 1g dry yeast

Sauce

  • 1 can of whole peeled tomatoes
  • salt
  • fresh basil
  • olive oil

Toppings

  • mozzarella cheese
  • parmesan cheese
  • fresh basil
  • olive oil
  • everything you like

Steps

  1. Take a large container with a lid and put all ingridints for the pre-dough in it
  2. Seal the container with the lid and shake it. The flour and water should come togehter but not be fully mixed. In the end it should have a flaky consistence and there should be no unbound flour.
  3. Let the pre-dough rest for 1 hours at room temperature. After that put it in the fridge and let it rest for 24 - 48 hours
  4. Mix salt, water and yeast. Put the pre-dough in the kneading machine and add the salt-water-yeast-mixture little by little. Knead the dough until its smooth. Approximately 10-20 minutes
