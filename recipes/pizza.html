Pizza

Back to recipes overview

Description

Pizza is a delicious dish that is loved by many people. It is a flatbread that is topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings such as pepperoni, mushrooms, and olives. Pizza is typically baked in an oven and is a popular dish in many countries around the world. It is often served as a quick and easy meal and is perfect for sharing with friends and family.

I discovered that it takes not very much effort to make a very good pizza at home. The dough is easy to make and can be customized with your favorite toppings. You can also experiment with different types of cheese and sauces to create your own unique pizza recipe. I hope you enjoy making and eating this delicious dish as much as I do!

Ingredients

Pre-Dough

  • 1kg 00 flour
  • 500ml water
  • 5g dry yeast

Final Dough

  • Pre-Dough
  • 200ml water
  • 20g salt
  • 1g dry yeast

Sauce

  • 1 can of whole peeled tomatoes
  • salt
  • fresh basil
  • olive oil

Toppings

  • mozzarella cheese
  • parmesan cheese
  • fresh basil
  • olive oil
  • everything you like

Steps

  1. Take a large container with a lid and put all the ingredients for the pre-dough in it.
  2. Seal the container with the lid and shake it. The flour and water should come together, but not be fully mixed. In the end, it should have a flaky consistence and there should be no unbound flour.
  3. Let the pre-dough rest at room temperature for 1 hour. After that, put it in the fridge and let it rest for 24 - 48 hours.
  4. Mix salt, water and yeast. Put the pre-dough in the kneading machine and add the salt-water-yeast-mixture little by little. Knead the dough until it's smooth. Approximately 10-20 minutes. You can knead by hand, but this will take longer and it's not easy to incorporate the water in the pre-dough.
  5. Put the dough on the table and put some olive oil on your hands. Now stretch the dough and fold it. Repeat this step 3-4 times. After that, form a ball and let it rest for 1 hour.
  6. Preheat the oven to 300Â°C (or as hot as possible). Put a baking stone in the oven.
  7. Split the dough into 5 equal parts and shape each into a small ball.
  8. Place the 5 balls in a box with approximately 4 fingers widths between them. Cover the box with a lid and let the dough balls rest for another hour.
  9. Put flour on a plate until it's filled. Sprinkle flour over the dough you want to use. Take the dough and put it on the plate and cover it on both sides with a lot of flour. Transfer the dough to the table. Now stretch the dough with your hands.
  10. Put the sauce on the dough and add the cheese and the toppings you like.
  11. Put the pizza on a pizza shovel and put the pizza in the oven. Bake it for 5-10 minutes. The pizza is ready when the cheese is melted and the crust is golden brown.
